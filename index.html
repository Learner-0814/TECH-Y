Computer Shop

WELCOME! TECH-Y is a computer shop offering a variety of computers to our partners and customers alike!

TECH-Y offers a multitude of computers varying in power for different use cases such as:

  • General use (Browsing) - For the general public who only needs the most basic use such as Google inquiries, watching Youtube, scrolling Facebook and/or Instagram. General use will also be fast enough and responsive so that users will be able to do important tasks such as online work, homework or projects for convinience.
  • Gaming - Computers for gaming has been specially equipped with powerful specs to handle demanding games and players getting the highest amount of frames for smooth gameplay during online matches in games such as CS:GO, Valorant, Marvel Rivals and many other games available.
